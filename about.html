About Vilo Website Development

We're not just another web design company — we're your creative partner. Vilo was founded with one goal: to help passionate entrepreneurs like you succeed online with clean, custom websites that convert.

Our Story

Vilo was created by Arthur Rattray — a resilient developer who overcame two brain surgeries and turned recovery into purpose. What started as a creative outlet became a full-service website development company built to empower others.

Today, Vilo helps small businesses, startups, and brands bring their vision to life online. Every project reflects our grit, creativity, and commitment to helping others grow.

Our Values

  • Creativity – Each site we build is one-of-a-kind.
  • Reliability – We follow through, communicate clearly, and meet deadlines.
  • Impact – We design with purpose, strategy, and ROI in mind.
